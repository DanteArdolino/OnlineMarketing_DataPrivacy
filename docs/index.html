How You Are Tracked Online

Online advertising, inescapable due to the wealth of data readily available on each and every user. A single site can contain trackers that are hidden that take the smallest bits of data.

How You Are Tracked Online
Data Collection

When browsing the internet, digital traces are left behind that websites can legally use to keep track of your activities and identify you. The data collected can include; your location, what device you’re using, which advertisements you’ve clicked on, and more.

How to Remain Anonymous

Google, Facebook, the government, as well as your internet service provider (ISP) all track and sell data. This data is sold to third-party marketers and there then becomes a risk of security surrounding personal information.

Browser Fingerprints

The so called fingerprint of a browser is a unique ID that is generated that can be used to identify your particular browser on the computer.
